# LocalPost AI — Visual Direction

## Editorial Local-Brand Studio

LocalPost AI should feel like a calm, crafted studio for local-business storytelling rather than a generic SaaS admin panel. The interface combines a **deep forest-green shell**, a **warm cream workspace**, **fine paper-grid surfaces**, **mint action accents**, **soft peach highlights**, and **refined serif display headlines**. Practical controls use a highly legible sans-serif so editorial moments and operational work remain distinct.

The primary action color is forest green on light surfaces and mint inside the dark shell. Mint also indicates active navigation and positive readiness. Peach is decorative and reserved for warmth, not primary actions. Status colors remain semantic and restrained.

Every empty state should still show product value through branded artifacts such as palette chips, calendar tiles, post cards, or profile lines. Copy should be specific to website-derived brand analysis, locally grounded Google Business Profile posts, and month-by-month editorial control.

Motion should remain subtle and fast: small press feedback on buttons, short opacity/transform entrances, modest card lift on hover, and no distracting looping animation beyond active progress indicators.
